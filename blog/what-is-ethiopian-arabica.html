What is Ethiopian Arabica?

Ethiopia is widely regarded as the genetic birthplace of Arabica coffee. Farmers work across diverse elevations and microclimates, which helps explain why Ethiopian coffees can taste wildly different from one lot to the next — jasmine and tea-like cups in some areas, winey berries in others.

Arabica vs Robusta (the one-minute version)

Arabica typically has more sugars and lipids, often presenting as complexity and sweetness in the cup. Robusta can be higher in caffeine and more bitter; it has its place but is not the focus of Coffee+ blends.
