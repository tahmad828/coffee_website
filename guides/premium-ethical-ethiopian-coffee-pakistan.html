Why Ethiopian Arabica: Quality Meets Ethics

Ethiopia is the birthplace of coffee, and its high-altitude regions produce Arabica beans renowned for complex flavor profiles — think floral jasmine, bright citrus, or deep chocolate — alongside naturally lower acidity. For professionals, this means a smoother, more enjoyable cup that's easier to drink consistently throughout the workday.

But flavor is only half the story. Ethiopian smallholder farming communities often face challenges like price volatility and limited market access. Coffee+ partners directly with cooperatives that prioritize:

  • Fair compensation: Prices that cover sustainable farming costs and support community development
  • Environmental stewardship: Shade-grown practices that protect biodiversity and soil health
  • Transparency: Clear documentation of origin, processing, and pricing — no black boxes

When you choose Coffee+, you're not just buying beans — you're supporting a supply chain built on respect, not exploitation.
