coffee+

Coffee+ is more than a cup of coffee. It is a purpose-led initiative created to turn everyday moments into meaningful impact. With every purchase, Coffee+ supports year-round giving, helping fund vital humanitarian efforts while delivering coffee you can feel good about choosing.

Explore coffee for studying in Islamabad, coffee for coding, and premium ethical Ethiopian coffee in Pakistan — then shop blends.

We believe that doing good should be simple, consistent, and part of everyday life. That's why Coffee+ was created to make impact accessible, one sip at a time.

Our Coffee

Our blends are crafted using premium Ethiopian Arabica beans, sourced responsibly from farming communities known for their rich coffee heritage and exceptional quality. Every roast is selected to deliver depth of flavour while honouring the people and places behind each harvest.

Our Coffee Range

coffee+ ENERGIZER ⚡

Bold | Uplifting | Full-bodied

Energizer is crafted for those who like their coffee strong, rich, and motivating. With a deeper roast profile, this blend delivers a robust flavour and a powerful finish, making it the perfect companion for early mornings and moments that call for focus and momentum.

coffee+ FANTASY

Smooth | Aromatic | Light-bodied

Fantasy is a lighter, more delicate blend designed to awaken the senses. With smooth notes and a gentle aroma, it offers an easy, refined cup that's ideal for relaxed moments, creative thinking, or a slow start to the day.

coffee+ //OSTALGIA

Balanced | Comforting | Classic

Nostalgia brings together balance and familiarity in every sip. This medium roast is warm, smooth, and approachable, delivering a comforting cup that feels timeless. It's the kind of coffee you reach for again and again, any time of day.
