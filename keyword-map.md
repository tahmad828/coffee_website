## Coffee+ keyword-to-page map (v1)

This mapping enforces **1 primary keyword per page** to avoid cannibalization. Secondary keywords should support the same intent.

### Global rules
- **PDPs**: brand + blend name keywords; support “Ethiopian Arabica”, roast level, taste notes, and “buy/order online”.
- **Hub pages**: persona intent pages (students / IT / working pros) that link to relevant PDPs and 2–4 supporting blog posts.
- **Blog posts**: informational intent only; must link to the hub + at least 1 PDP.
