Coffee+ — white-hat outreach checklist (off-page pillar)
=======================================================

Linkable assets (host on site first):
- /impact.html — Giving Coffee Initiative + partner transparency
- /blog/what-is-ethiopian-arabica.html — origin education (easy to cite)
- /guides/premium-ethical-ethiopian-coffee-pakistan.html — ethical + local intent

Prospecting (Islamabad / Rawalpindi + Pakistan digital):
- Student societies / campus newsletters (study focus angle)
- Tech meetups, dev communities, coworking blogs (coding productivity angle)
- Lifestyle / food bloggers (ethical Ethiopian + delivery)

Outreach rules:
- Personalised email: reference their recent article or audience
- Ask for editorial consideration, not “link insertion”
- Disclose any sponsorship; paid links must use rel="sponsored" (or nofollow)

Monthly KPI loop (Search Console + GA4):
- Top queries by impression: improve title/meta/FAQ for CTR
- Landing pages with high traffic + low conversion: strengthen CTA + PDP links
- New content: 2+ internal links from existing hubs/PDPs
