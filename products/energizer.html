Energizer — focus-forward Ethiopian coffee

Bold | Uplifting | Full-bodied

Energizer is a medium-dark roast Ethiopian Arabica built for mornings, study blocks, and “need clarity now” moments. Expect a robust body, motivating finish, and a cup that pairs well with Aeropress, cafetière, or drip.

Who it’s for: students, founders, and anyone who wants a confident cup without fluff. See the study guide for Islamabad.

Impact: purchases support the Giving Coffee Initiative.

$18.99

Also explore Fantasy for a lighter creative cup.

Brewing suggestions

  • 1:15 coffee-to-water as a starting ratio for drip; adjust to taste.
  • Fresh grind right before brewing preserves aromatics for focus sessions.
  • Let it cool slightly — sweetness opens up a few degrees below boiling.

Product FAQ

Is Energizer high caffeine?

It’s a bold profile; perceived “strength” comes from roast and extraction as much as caffeine. If you’re sensitive, start with a smaller cup.
