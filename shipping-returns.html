Shipping & Returns

Shipping: Processing times, courier partners, and delivery coverage may vary by season and address. Confirm your sector (Islamabad, Rawalpindi, or other) at checkout or via Contact before bulk orders.

Returns: If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos and your order reference. We will replace or refund eligible items per policy in force at the time of purchase.

Freshness: Coffee is a food product; opened bags may be non-returnable except where required by law or where quality is demonstrably defective.
